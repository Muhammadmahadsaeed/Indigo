DESERT STUDIO

//ABOUT

Desert Studio is a fully integrated textile set-up, established in 2013 in Dubai. The studio is created out of the desire to create a new, modern and better way for denim manufacturing. Desert studio provides high-end customers with premium quality denim fabrics and garments.

Desert Studio’s strength lies in its unique capacity to combine its experience in wash recipes, fabric development, and fit perfection.

The Studio continually strives to do better. The set-up in Dubai creates the most beautiful garments and fabrics while continuously experimenting in the search for more efficient and more sustainable manufacturing.

We value a strong long-term partnership with our customers to enable us to provide them with the highest level of service.

Desert Studio is passionate about denim and sets an entirely new standard when it comes to sustainability, development, and service.
