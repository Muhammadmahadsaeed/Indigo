DESERT STUDIO// LASER

In the past years Desert Studio turned its focus to creating ethical, sustainable and eco-efficient ways of denim manufacturing. To do so we added 5 laser machines from Jeanologia to our hydroless laundry.

By adding these laser machines we can produce totally environment-friendly with comparatively negligible use of water with no use of chemicals.

Besides the environmental advantages the laser ensures more accurate, precise detailing and it saves the denim fabric from damages caused by traditional chemicals, stones and bleaches.
